Speaking Buddies

Here at speaking buddies, we give underprivileged students
a safe place to practice their English. We offer students the chance to
speak with fluent speakers in hopes that English can open doors of
opportunities for them. We work with very determined and disciplined
students and with the help of us and our volunteers we hope to touch the
lives of many more. Our hope is that we can help these students foster the
ability to speak confidently and fluently whenever and wherever they are.We
know that with the thirty minute calls our volunteers and buddies will both
be able to enjoy and learn from this opportunity.
