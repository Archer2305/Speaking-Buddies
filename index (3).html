How it started



COVID-19 has impacted many around the world; everyone's community life, social life,
and daily interaction is now gone. This meant the possibility to interact with a fluent
English speaker for these underprivileged students would be even harder. After connecting
with students, we were seeing that although many of the students were putting in hour
after an hour into learning English, they had no one to practice with. With this, we realized
that since English is our first language why not carry an interesting and smooth conversation
which would be a fun way to practice new English? And from there it has blossomed into the
organization we are now.
