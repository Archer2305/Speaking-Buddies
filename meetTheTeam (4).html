Meet the Team!

These are the people who have made speaking buddies possible...




 
CEO and Founder

Hey there! My name is Maanya Asuti and I am a junior at Dougherty Valley High School. From my own personal experience, I have seen how far the ability to speak English can improve one’s quality of life, but I have also seen how underprivileged students do not have the luxury of practicing English no matter how determined they are. This made me realize that although these students may have learned the basics of English they do not have the means and resources to practice their English. I started this organization to help low-income students improve their conversational English skills in hope that one day they have the chance to escape poverty and build a new life.





Co-Founder

My name is Yash Austi and I am an 8th grader at Gale Ranch Middle School. From day one of Speaking Buddies, I have been working to coordinate and answer the questions from all of our volunteers. I love the idea that in just 30 minutes, we can make a real difference in someone's life, helping them learn conversational skills and other essential English skills to help better their lives.
